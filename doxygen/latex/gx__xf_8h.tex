enum \mbox{\hyperlink{gx__xf_8h_add852a47f0d13113c705c6cee99b1d23}{gx\+\_\+mtx\+\_\+id\+\_\+t}}}



Universal matrix ids. 

These are used for addressing geometry, normal, texcoord matrices and dual texcoord matrices.

Depending on what of these its for, some or all of these may be valid.

Geometry and texcoord share the same memory, and use GX\+\_\+\+MTX\+\_\+\+ID\+\_\+0 through GX\+\_\+\+MTX\+\_\+\+ID\+\_\+20. Where GX\+\_\+\+MTX\+\_\+\+ID\+\_\+20 by default is loaded as an idiniticy matrix for them to use.

Normal matrices can only use GX\+\_\+\+MTX\+\_\+\+ID\+\_\+0 through GX\+\_\+\+MTX\+\_\+\+ID\+\_\+9.

Dual texcoords have their own stack and can use GX\+\_\+\+MTX\+\_\+\+ID\+\_\+0, GX\+\_\+\+MTX\+\_\+\+ID\+\_\+20.
